Footer at the bottom of a short page

On my shortest page, which is Contact, the footer had white space below it. It is not my first time facing this problem and I thought I knew how to fix this.

What caused the problem?

The reason is simple: there is not enough content on the page to push the footer to the bottom and therefore the space under it was left blank.

How was it fixed?

I remembered that on the course we were taught to use min-height: 100vh that would stretch the container vertically and not leave any white space. I applied it to html but that was not it.

Then I googled my question and got an idea of using position: relative / absolute on the footer and html, and not forgetting to set bottom and left to 0. It almost fixed everything but now the footer was on top of some of the content.

The last thing that I needed to do was setting margin-bottom of the body to the height of the footer ( + some margin but it depends on the code).

Problem solved.
