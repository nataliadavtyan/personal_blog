Hi! My name is Natalia and welcome to my learning journal

After several months of learning in the Scrimba's Frontend Developer Career Path I was able to create a 10-page blog and gained more confidence in my coding skills.

Why did I choose frontend?

Before getting into frontend development I was (and still am) a photographer, a publisher of a lifestyle magazine and a brand manager and some of my other interests are climate change and gender equality. I am passionate about seeing and changing the environment around me. Now I would like to collaborate with other people and build digital environment.

How did I know I would like coding?

At school I loved playing with numbers and had creative approach to problem solving at Math. Moreover, we had computer classes and sometimes we were programming a game within a programming game which I also enjoyed.

It was difficult for me in the beginning, especially with JavaScript, but the more I code, the more confidence I gain.

I am very excited to continue the course and learn more.
