Planning before starting to code

This is my first project with multiple HTML files and not planning the process made me spend more time then I expected.

What happened?

This blog has 10 pages in total and 1 CSS file. The difficult part was carefully observing the provided Figma design file and finding similarities between objects and pages so they could be grouped together in one class.

What made things even more difficult is that mobile and desktop version have differences and I didn't observe them carefully as I was thinking about the mobile-first approach. As a result I spent six days coding this blog and constantly rewriting it.

What did I learn?

It is not something new for me but now I know where I need to pay more attention. As a person with a photography background I know that the speed and quality of a photoshoot often depend on how well I was prepared for it.
